Win With Black Doctors(#WWBD)

About #WWBD

Win With Black Doctors (WWBD) was born out of a powerful call to action during the historic Win With Black Women (WWBW) Zoom call, which brought together over 44,000 Black women on the day President Biden endorsed Kamala Harris. I was one of those 44,000+ participants, deeply moved and inspired to take action. Soon after, I gathered a team of passionate healthcare leaders to support Kamala Harris's presidential campaign. Together, we launched WWBD as a sister initiative to WWBW, extending its mission to Black doctors and allies under the banner of "Doctors for Kamala."

Join the Movement

Our Mission

  • Endorse the Harris-Walz ticket and actively support "Doctors for Kamala" through events, fundraising, and phone banking.
  • Access tools to help their patients register to vote (in collaboration with Vot-ER).
  • Stay connected to reliable fact-checking resources on candidates' health policies.
  • Raise awareness of the detrimental impact Project 2025 could have on health equity.
  • Support minority health professionals running for public office across the country.
  • Hold any administration accountable to improving healthcare workforce wellbeing.

Our Campaigns

#NotStayingNeutral

Encourages health professionals to speak out about the serious implications of Project 2025 and the critical stakes of this campaign.

#The5thVitalSign

Aims to help patients understand the connection between their health and civic engagement. Commit to a minimum of 47 patient encounters discussing this and share your experience and insight with the group by tagging us.
